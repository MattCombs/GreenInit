the Problem

Baltimore is in need of great change. The city itself is beautiful and filled with amazing, creative people. It is a hub for innovation - the “silicon valley” of the east coast. Yet Baltimore has been painted red by the media. They irritate the dark times in Baltimore’s past and leave no time for growth and healing. Baltimore, just like any city, has its scars, but now is the time to enact change and empower Baltimore to be ranked number one for greenest city, as well as the most sustainable city in America. If you want the young entrepreneurs and innovators to swarm your city? There is no better way than to be a leader in sustainability.
